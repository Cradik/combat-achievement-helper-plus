# Combat Achievement Helper+

## Mission

Never let a player wonder what Combat Achievement to do next.

---

## Philosophy

- Know What's Next.
- Remove Decision Fatigue.
- Celebrate the Player.
- Make RuneLite feel native.
- Every click should feel rewarding.
- Earn Version 2.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Combat Achievement Helper+ begins as the best Combat Achievement companion for RuneLite.

Over time it may evolve into a complete progression companion for Old School RuneScape while always staying true to its mission:

Know what's next.
